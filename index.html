About Me

I am a multidisciplinary technology professional working across data science, analytics, cloud engineering, AI/ML, and backend development, with a strong focus on building systems that move from raw data to real-world impact.

My work spans the entire data and application lifecycle—from data acquisition, cleaning, and exploratory analysis, to machine learning, AI system design, deployment, and backend integration. I specialize in turning unstructured and structured data into decision-ready insights, intelligent automation, and scalable production systems.

On the data side, I work extensively with Python, SQL, and R for statistical analysis, predictive modeling, time series forecasting, and business analytics. I design interactive dashboards and reporting systems using Power BI, Tableau, Streamlit, and Excel, enabling stakeholders to explore performance, trends, and operational drivers with clarity.

In AI and machine learning, my experience includes classical ML, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). I have built and deployed AI systems involving LLM fine-tuning, vector search, agentic workflows, and multimodal document understanding, with a strong emphasis on evaluation, reliability, and production readiness.

From an engineering and cloud perspective, I design and deploy scalable backend services and data pipelines using FastAPI, Django, Flask, Laravel, RESTful APIs, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines. I work comfortably across AWS, Azure, GCP, and IBM Cloud, delivering systems that are secure, maintainable, and aligned with business constraints.

What defines my approach is systems thinking—the ability to connect data, models, infrastructure, and applications into cohesive solutions that solve real problems, not just technical exercises.
